Basic Geometric Properties and Polygons

  • Geometry includes points,lines,angles,shaped and solids
Learning Objectives
  • State what acute, right, obtuse, reflex, complimentary and supplementary angles are and give examples for each.
  • Apply the right properties involving parallel lines
  • State the formula for finding the sum of interior angles of polygons
  • State the formula for finding sum of exterior angles of polygons
    • Types of angles
  • Acute angle is an angle that is less than `90` degrees
  • Right angle is an angle equivalent to `90` degrees
  • Obtuse angle is an angle between `90` degrees and `180` degrees (not inclusive)
  • Reflex angle is an angle between `180` degrees and `360` degrees (not inclusive)

    Polygons
  • A polygon is a closed plane figure with three or more straight line segments as its sides
  • A regular polygon is a polygon with all sides equal and all angles equal
  • The sum of interior angles of a n-sided polygon = `(n-2) xx 180^@`
  • The interior angle of a regular n-sided polygon = `((n-2) xx 180^@)/n`
